# Follow-Up Template

**When to use:** After a demo, after sending a proposal, or after any meeting where Matt committed to a next step.  
**Length target:** Short. 50-80 words.  
**Tone:** Warm, efficient. Assumes they're busy.

---

## Template

**Subject:** Re: [original thread subject] OR [Meeting/Demo recap]

[One line referencing what you covered — specific to the meeting, not generic]

[2-3 sentences: the key takeaway, the agreed next step, and anything you're sending or following up on]

[Clear ask or next step — specific date/time if possible]

Matt

---

## Post-demo follow-up example

Subject: Re: Vibes demo — next steps

[Name], thanks for the time today. Sounds like the SMS loyalty use case resonated most.

Sending over the [case study / ROI model / proposal] we discussed — will have it to you by [day].

Does [specific date/time] work for a follow-up call with [whoever was missing from the first call]?

Matt

---

## Stale deal follow-up example

Subject: [Company] + Vibes — checking in

[Name], following up from our conversation [timeframe]. 

[One sentence referencing something that's happened since — their news, a relevant Vibes client story, something specific.]

Still worth a conversation?

Matt
