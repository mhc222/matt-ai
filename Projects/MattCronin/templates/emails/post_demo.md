# Post-Demo Email Template

**When to use:** Within 24 hours of a demo or discovery call.  
**Length target:** 100-150 words.  
**Tone:** Professional, action-oriented. Make the next step easy.

---

## Template

**Subject:** [Company] + Vibes — [date] recap

[Name],

Thanks for the time today. [One specific thing that came up in the conversation — a pain point, an insight they shared, a use case that resonated.]

[What you're following up with — resources, a case study, a specific proposal item. Be concrete.]

[Proposed next step — date, who should be on the call, what you'll cover.]

Let me know if you have questions before then.

Matt

---

## Example

Subject: ACME Corp + Vibes — June 16 recap

Sarah,

Thanks for the time today. The wallet pass integration for your loyalty program is a strong fit — particularly the contactless point redemption at POS.

Sending over the Target case study and a rough ROI model based on your current email-to-SMS conversion rates. Give me until EOD tomorrow.

Would [date] work for a follow-up with your IT lead to walk through the integration scope? Usually 45 minutes.

Matt
