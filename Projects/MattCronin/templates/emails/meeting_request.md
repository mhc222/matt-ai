# Meeting Request Template

**When to use:** Asking for an intro/discovery call after some initial context has been established (replied to an email, connected on LinkedIn, was referred, etc.)  
**Length target:** Very short. Under 60 words.  
**Tone:** Confident, respectful of their time.

---

## Template

**Subject:** [15 or 20] minutes — [brief hook]

[One line of context — why you're reaching out, what the connection is]

[One line on what the call is about — specific value to them, not a "see if there's a fit" boilerplate]

Worth [15/20] minutes on [specific day(s)]?

Matt

---

## Example

Subject: 20 minutes — Vibes + [Company] mobile engagement

[Referral name] suggested I reach out — said you're ramping up SMS as a loyalty channel this year.

Happy to share what's working for [comparable brand] — 15-min call this week or next?

Matt

---

## Drafting notes for Claude

- Never ask for "30 minutes" — 15 or 20 reads as lower commitment
- Include a specific reason why now (trigger event, referral, relevant case study)
- Offer specific days rather than "whenever works for you"
- Do not include a Calendly link in cold outreach — save that for warm follow-up
