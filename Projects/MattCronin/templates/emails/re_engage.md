# Re-Engagement Template

**When to use:** A deal or prospect has gone cold (60+ days no response). Last attempt before moving to nurture.  
**Length target:** Very short. Under 50 words.  
**Tone:** Not desperate. Low pressure. Leave the door open.

---

## Template

**Subject:** [Short, slightly unexpected subject — not "following up again"]

[One line — a genuine reason to resurface, a new piece of news, or honest acknowledgment that timing may not have been right]

Still on your radar, or should I check back in [timeframe]?

Matt

---

## Examples

**Approach A — news hook:**

Subject: [Company] + [recent relevant news]

[Name], saw [Company] just [launched app / hired new CMO / announced expansion]. 

Thought it might make Vibes worth another look. Still interested, or should I check back in Q4?

Matt

---

**Approach B — direct and honest:**

Subject: Timing

[Name], I'll be honest — I'm not sure if the timing was off or if the priority shifted.

Either way, happy to pick back up when it makes sense. Just let me know.

Matt

---

## Drafting notes for Claude

- Research the company for any trigger events before deciding on the approach
- If no news hook exists, Approach B (honest/direct) usually outperforms a generic "checking in"
- If this email also gets no response, move the deal to Closed Lost and remove from active outreach
- Do not send a re-engagement email more than once per quarter to the same person
