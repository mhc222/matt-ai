# Cold Outreach Template

**When to use:** First touch to a prospect with no prior relationship.  
**Length target:** Under 100 words.  
**Tone:** Warm, specific, no pitch dump.

---

## Template

**Subject:** [Specific hook — reference their company, a trigger event, or a shared context]

[First line: one sentence referencing something specific about them or their company. Not "I hope this email finds you well."]

[2-3 sentences max: what Vibes does, framed around their world — not a feature list. Reference a problem or trend that applies to them.]

[Soft close: not "can we jump on a call?" — something lower friction like asking a question or offering a specific resource.]

[Sign-off]  
Matt
