## Platform Channels — What They Are and Why They Matter

### SMS and MMS
**What it does:** Sends personalized, time-sensitive messages directly to customers' phones.

**Why it matters:** High visibility, fast customer action, strong ROI, direct path to customers.

**Best use cases:** Promotions, flash sales, loyalty reminders, cart recovery, service alerts, event reminders, personalized offers.

---

### RCS (Rich Communication Services)
**What it does:** Rich media messaging with images, buttons, carousels, branded experiences, and interactive calls to action — delivered natively in the messages app.

**Why it matters:** More visual storytelling, higher engagement, better product discovery, branded customer journeys without requiring an app.

**Best use cases:** Rich promotions, product galleries, cart recovery with images and checkout buttons, loyalty updates, upsell/cross-sell, branded customer journeys.

---

### Mobile Wallet
**What it does:** Creates digital passes for offers, coupons, rewards, loyalty cards, and updates delivered through Apple Wallet and Google Wallet.

**Why it matters:**
- Bridges online and in-store experiences
- No app download required
- Keeps the brand present on the customer's phone persistently
- Supports real-time pass updates
- Helps close the attribution gap between digital and in-store

**Best use cases:** Loyalty cards, coupons, digital offers, rewards passes, event passes, renewal reminders, location-triggered promotions, in-store traffic campaigns.

**Key stat:** SMS + Mobile Wallet campaigns drive 19x more revenue per message than SMS alone.

---

### Push and App Inbox
**What it does:** Reaches customers who have the brand's app installed with targeted notifications and in-app messages.

**Why it matters:** Powerful for already-engaged app users, but limited by app adoption rates.

## How the Platform Works (Agent-Friendly Summary)

1. **Data in** — Customer and loyalty data enter via CRM, CDP, loyalty, ecommerce, or APIs
2. **Audience selection** — Segment by demographics, loyalty tier, behavior, purchase history
3. **Channel selection** — Choose SMS, MMS, RCS, Wallet, or Push based on goal and device capability
4. **Campaign build** — Personalized messages via templates, AI recommendations, dynamic fields
5. **Automation and delivery** — Scheduled or behavior-triggered
6. **Customer engagement** — Click, redeem, save to wallet, purchase, book
7. **Reporting** — Real-time dashboards for attribution, delivery, ROI
8. **Optimization** — Insights improve future campaigns
